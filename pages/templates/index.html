Welcome Tigers

Welcome runners, whether you're looking to continue racing in college or just stay in shape, we're the club for you! We welcome all runners and strive to improve both ourselves and those around us!

WHO WE ARE

The Clemson Running Club is the official running club of the University. We are a student organization under the Club Sports umbrella and are open to any full time Clemson student, including graduate and Bridge students.
